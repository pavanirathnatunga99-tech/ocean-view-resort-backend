Help & User Guide

Follow the steps below to efficiently use the Ocean View Resort Management System.

🔐 User Authentication

Use your registered username and password to log in securely. If you are a new user, create an account before accessing the dashboard.

🏨 Add New Reservation

Navigate to the Reservation section and enter: Guest Name, Address, Contact Number, Room Type, Check-in Date, and Check-out Date. Click "Save Reservation" to generate a unique Reservation ID.

📋 View Reservation Details

Enter the Reservation ID in the View section to retrieve complete booking information including guest details and stay duration.

💰 Generate & Print Bill

Use the Reservation ID to calculate the total cost. The system automatically computes the number of nights based on check-in and check-out dates. Click "Print Bill" to download or print the invoice as PDF.

🚪 Exit System

Click the Exit option in the sidebar to safely log out and close the management session.
